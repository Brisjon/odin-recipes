Crab Cakes

Crab, bread crumbs, and egg formed into patties then fried to a golden brown

Crab Cakes are a cooked dish. It is prepared using either the kitchen inside an upgraded farmhouse or using a Cookout Kit.

Ingredients

  • Crab (1)
  • Wheat Flour (1)
  • Egg (1)
  • Oil (1)

Steps

  1. Catch one crab using a Crab Pot from any saltwater body of water
  2. Purchase one Wheat Flour from Pierre's General Store for 125g
  3. Collect one Egg produced from any of the chickens you may have in the Chicken Coop
  4. Purchase one Oil from Pierre's General Store for 200g
  5. Combine the ingredients at the kitchen inside your farmhouse, or alternatively using a Cookout Kit
