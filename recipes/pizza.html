Pizza

It's popular for all the right reasons

Pizza is a cooked dish. It is prepared using either the kitchen inside an upgraded farmhouse or using a Cookout Kit.

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour (1)
  • Tomato (1)
  • Cheese (1)

Steps

  1. Purchase one wheat flour from Pierre's General Storefor 125g
  2. Harvest one tomato (grown from tomato seeds after 11 days)
  3. Using milk to produce normal quality cheese,harvest one cheese from the Cheese Press
  4. Combine the ingredients at the kitchen inside your farmhouse, or alternatively using a Cookout Kit
