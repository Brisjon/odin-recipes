Salad

A healthy garden salad

Salad is a cooked dish. It is prepared using either the kitchen inside an upgraded farmhouse or using a Cookout Kit.

Ingredients

  • Leek (1)
  • Dandelion (1)
  • Vinegar (1)

Steps

  1. Forage one leek during Spring-time, rarely found in the Mountains and Backwoods
  2. Forage one Dandelion during Spring-time,commonly found everywhere
  3. Purchase one Vinegar from Pierre's General Store for 200g
  4. Combine the ingredients at the kitchen inside your farmhouse, or alternatively using a Cookout Kit
